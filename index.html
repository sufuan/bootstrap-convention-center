Frequently Asked Questions

If the LACC lots are full, there is plenty of parking nearby. Please refer to the map for all available lots surrounding the LACC.

Daily parking rate ranges $15-30; due to demand and location, the fees vary between parking garages and events. Feel free to inquire about a specific event's pricing.
NOTE: Accessible parking and motorcycle parking are also subject to the same fees.

The LACC does not permit outside food and beverage. While an individual item i.e. bottle of water is ok, Items in bulk such as a case of water or a cooler with food and snacks is not allowed on property and will have to be consumed outside of the venue. Please contact the event organizer if you have further questions.

Our general parking hours are 5:30am - 9:00pm.
